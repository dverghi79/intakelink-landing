How It Works

The four-step intake that actually closes deals

1

Client uploads documents

You send one branded link. Your client fills out their immigration details, uploads passport, visa, tax returns, and any supporting documents. No account, no hassle. The form validates file format and completeness in real time.

2

IntakeLink verifies completeness

The system checks that all required documents are present and correctly formatted. If something's missing or wrong, the client sees exactly what needs to be fixed before they can submit. No email back-and-forth.

3

Engagement letter auto-generates

The client's intake data prepopulates your engagement letter template. It appears in the same browser window, ready for e-signature. Your client reads it and signs without leaving the intake flow.

4

LawPay retainer collected

Once the engagement letter is signed, a LawPay payment request appears for your standard retainer. Payment goes straight to your account. You get a Slack notification that the case is funded and ready to open in Clio.
